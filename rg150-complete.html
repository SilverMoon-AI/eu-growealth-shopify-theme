Growealth RG150 LED Grow Light

The Growealth RG150 is a compact and efficient 150W full spectrum LED grow light designed for indoor cultivation environments such as grow tents, cloning stations, and nursery racks. With advanced full-spectrum LEDs and flexible dimming options, the RG150 delivers reliable light output for every stage of plant growth.

Key Features

  • Full Spectrum + UV & IR – Optimized spectrum for seedling, vegetative and flowering stages.
  • High Efficiency – Up to 2.5 μmol/J PPE for strong plant growth with lower energy consumption.
  • Manual Knob + 0–10V Dimming – Easily adjust light intensity or connect to external controllers.
  • Daisy Chain Control – Multiple fixtures can be connected for synchronized dimming.
  • Ultra-Thin Integrated Design – Lightweight and easy to install in compact growing spaces.
  • Passive Cooling – Fanless aluminum design for silent and reliable operation.

Application

The RG150 is ideal for small grow tents, cloning rooms, nursery racks, and mother plant cultivation. Its compact form factor allows growers to maximize light coverage in limited spaces while maintaining excellent plant development.
